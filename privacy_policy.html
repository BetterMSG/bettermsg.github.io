Privacy Policy

for Message ID+ (Messaging • Caller ID • Spam Block)

We respect your privacy. This Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and the choices available to you when using Message ID+ (the “App”, “we”, “our”, “us”).

1) Scope

This Policy applies to all features of the App, including messaging, caller identification, spam blocking, in-app settings, diagnostics, and customer support.

2) Permissions & Purpose (Android)

We request only the minimum permissions necessary to deliver core functionality. You can manage or revoke any permission at any time in System Settings → Apps → Message ID+ → Permissions.

PermissionPurposeData Flow
Phone / Call Log Identify incoming callers, detect spam, show call details. Processed on-device; call metadata may be used for spam detection. No call audio is recorded.
SMS / MMS Send/receive messages, filter spam texts, provide messaging features (e.g., scheduling). Message content and attachments remain on-device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to back up or share.
Contacts Display names/avatars for calls and messages; enable group messaging. Contacts are read on-device only; not stored on our servers.
Notifications Show incoming call/message alerts and actions. Notification data is handled locally on-device.
Storage (Media Access) Let you attach photos/videos to messages or export logs you explicitly choose to save. Files remain under your control; the App accesses only items you select.

3) Information We Collect

A. Device & App Diagnostics

  • Device model, OS version, app version, performance metrics, crash logs.

B. Caller ID & Spam Protection

  • Call metadata (number, time, duration) used to identify unknown callers and detect spam/fraud.
  • Spam reports you submit (e.g., numbers you mark as spam). When shared to improve protection, they are anonymized.

C. Messaging

  • SMS/MMS content and attachments are processed locally to deliver messages and filter spam.

D. Support Communications

  • If you email us, we receive your email address and the message you send for customer support.

4) How We Use Information

  • Provide messaging, caller identification, and spam blocking.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to spam, fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Maintain, analyze, and improve app performance and reliability.
  • Respond to user inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

5) Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may be disclosed only in these limited situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors operating under confidentiality and data-processing agreements (e.g., secure hosting, diagnostics).
  • Legal Compliance: When required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Security & Safety: To detect, prevent, or address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.

6) Security

  • Data in transit is protected with industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Sensitive data such as messages, contacts, and call content is stored on your device by default.
  • We apply technical and organizational safeguards to reduce risks of unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7) Data Retention & Deletion

  • On-device data remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Diagnostic logs (if collected) are retained only as long as necessary for troubleshooting and service quality.
  • You may request deletion of information you shared with support by emailing us (see “Contact Us”).

8) Your Rights & Choices

  • Revoke permissions in device settings at any time without losing access to unrelated features.
  • Where available, disable analytics/diagnostics within the App settings.
  • Access, correct, or request deletion of support communications by contacting us.

9) Third-Party Services

The App may integrate trusted third-party services (e.g., diagnostics or crash reporting). Such providers process data under their own privacy policies and applicable data-protection laws.

10) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that such data has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the latest changes. Material changes will be communicated within the App or on this page.
